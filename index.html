Hello! I am a postdoctoral researcher and lecturer at Smith College. In July 2024, I will start a new position as an Assistant Professor in the Department of Mathematics and Statistics at Vassar College. My research interests are in topological data analysis (TDA), computational topology and geometry, network science, and mathematical biology. My pronouns are she/her.

Links: CV. google scholar. linkedin.
